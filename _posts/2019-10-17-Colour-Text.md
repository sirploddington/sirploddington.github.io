 To put a line of text in colour:
 
 ```csharp
 
 Console.ForegroundColor = ConsoleColor.Cyan;
 ```
 
 To reset that colour:
 
 ```csharp
 
Console.ResetColor();

 ```
 
 To put a background colour:
 
 ```csharp
 Console.BackgroundColor = ConsoleColor.Blue;
 
 ```
